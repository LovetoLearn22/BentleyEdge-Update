How Does BentleyEdge Work?

BentleyEdge partners with student-led groups, faculty, and alumni across Bentley University to capture and share their valuable content, events, meetings, and insights. We transform this wealth of knowledge into accessible articles, videos, forums, discussions, audios, and podcasts. Our mission is to translate and publish Bentley’s unique perspectives and achievements to the world, making education equitable and accessible for everyone.

BentleyEdge believes in providing a glimpse into Bentley’s vibrant academic and extracurricular ecosystem, offering those outside our community the opportunity to learn and grow with us. Through BentleyEdge, you can experience the pulse of Bentley University, engage with real-world insights, and be part of our journey towards knowledge and innovation.
